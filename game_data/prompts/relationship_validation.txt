# Relationship Validation Prompt Templates
# These templates are used during script validation to verify correct relationships

## TEMPLATE: Sibling Relationships Check

Verify the following sibling relationships are correctly portrayed:
- Allison and Tyler are SIBLINGS (brother and sister)
- Do NOT refer to them as "friends", "companions", "friendship", etc.
- CORRECT: "his sister", "her brother", "the twins"
- INCORRECT: "his friend", "her companion", "their friend"

## TEMPLATE: Parent-Child Relationships Check

Verify the following parent-child relationships:
- Mary Anne is the MOTHER of Allison and Tyler
- Do NOT use alternate names like "Mary Ann"
- CORRECT: "their mother", "Mary Anne", "the twins' mother"
- INCORRECT: "Mary Ann", "their guardian" (unless explicitly stated)

## TEMPLATE: Relationship Keywords

SIBLING_RELATIONSHIP_KEYWORDS: sibling, brother, sister, twins, twin, younger sister, older brother
PARENT_RELATIONSHIP_KEYWORDS: mother, father, parent, mom, dad, their mother, their father
FRIEND_RELATIONSHIP_KEYWORDS: friend, companion, buddy (use ONLY for non-family connections)

## EXAMPLE: Relationship Errors

ERROR: "Allison, his friend and companion"
FIX: "Allison, his twin sister"

ERROR: "Mary Ann"
FIX: "Mary Anne"

ERROR: "with Tyler, his best friend"
FIX: "with Tyler, her twin brother"
